Public Art Project Experience:

Ballard Locks Smolt Salmon Migration Improvements, Salmon Waves. Percent for Art commission awarded jointly by Seattle Arts Commission and King County Arts Commission. Completed 2001.

Pacific Fountain. Percent for Art commission awarded by King County Arts Commission. Completed 2004.

City of Auburn Transit Station Artwork, Catch the Train!. Percent for Art commission awarded by Auburn Arts Commission. Completed 2004.

Sustainability Award Artwork. Public commission awarded by Seattle Arts Commission and Sustainable Seattle, to create an educational interactive sculptural display that teaches the public about sustainable practices and serves as an award to recognize the annual recipients of the Sustainability Award. Completed 2004.

Tree Bench at Pratt Park. Public commission awarded by Seattle Office of Arts and Cultural Affairs to create a public artwork in an urban park in Seattle. Project to be installed November 2005.

Coffee Pot Pergola in Lake City. Project artist for $20,000 commission through Seattle Office of Arts and Cultural affairs to create a public artwork in conjunction with street improvements taking place along Lake City Way. Seattle, Washington. Completed 2005.

Longfellow Creek Trail Gateway. Design and fabrication of 12 gateway posts, in collaboration with Pomegranate Center and Longfellow Creek Community Group in West Seattle. Seattle, Washington, 2001.

Regional Justice Center, Front Entry Artwork, Justice Garden Path. Two-artist collaboration with Lorna Jordan. King County 1% for Art, project budget $250,000. Kent, Washington. 1995-1997.

Pacific Science Center, The Tech Zone. Flock, a computer-controlled kinetic sculpture installation, on permanent display, 1995 - present.

Cedar River Watershed Interpretive Center, North Bend, Washington, Seattle Arts Commission 1% for Art project: technical design and fabrication of Water Drums, a computer-controlled musical instrument and environmental art piece, for artist Dan Corson. 2001.

Woodinville City Hall, Flowering Structure, Outdoor site sculpture, one year installation, Woodinville, Washington. 2001-2002.

 

Education:
